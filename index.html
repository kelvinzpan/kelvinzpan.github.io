about me

My name is actually Zi Long, but I go by Kelvin. I am a part-time student, programmer, gamer, student leader, artist, and a full-time creative... unless I've been missing some sleep.

I'm exploring what is it I like within the realm of software development. My latest projects have been web-focused, and I'm currently teaching myself Node.js. I'm also interested in game development, so expect some Unity projects in the near future. When I'm not coding, I like to draw, play video games, and contribute to collegiate esports.

My current proficiencies are in: Java, C/C++, Python, HTML, CSS/SASS, and JavaScript. I could probably smash out a bit of Assembly, but it's been too long (for good reasons).
