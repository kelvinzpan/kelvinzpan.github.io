bear pong, a multiplayer game

Bear Pong is a 3D multiplayer video game written from scratch in C++ by a team of six. It plays like Rocket League, except the players are bears, it's themed after ping pong, and there are projectiles and explosions. More information and source code are located on this GitHub repo. Unfortunately, the live stream of our presentation was not saved. Hopefully it will be uploaded to YouTube within the next year.
